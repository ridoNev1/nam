About Us

Building global energy solutions from coconut shells

CV Nayyara Mitra Kalendra (NAM) is an Indonesian export partner specialising in coconut shell briquettes for shisha and barbecue markets. Since 2024 we have combined strategic sourcing, traceability, and customer-centric collaboration to help distributors, importers, and OEM brands scale their businesses.

Company Overview

Established in 2024 with a global mandate

Headquartered in Surabaya with a production centre in Sidoarjo, NAM delivers premium coconut shell briquettes to partners across Europe, the Middle East, Red Sea countries, Asia, and the Americas. We partner closely with CV Sinar Tirta Jaya to secure consistent raw materials and operate dual production lines with a combined capacity of up to 300 tons per month.

Anchored by permits and partnerships
