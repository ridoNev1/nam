Product Catalogue

Precision-crafted coconut shell briquettes

NAM manufactures cube, hexagonal, circle, flat, and Kaloud briquette formats from 100% coconut shells. Each batch is pressed, oven-dried, weathered, and tested to guarantee 7,500 Kcal performance, 2h30 burn time, and low ash levels demanded by premium lounges and grill operators.

Signature Range

Tailored formats for export-ready combustion

Each variant is pressed using custom moulds, dried in dedicated ovens, and cooled through a controlled weathering cycle to ensure stable density. Select the profile that aligns with your market or work with us on private-label dimensions.

Flagship 01

Cube 2.5 cm & 2.2 cm

Square cuts engineered for shisha lounges and BBQ markets. Crisp edges, steady airflow, and premium fixed carbon deliver stable heat for the full session.

  • 2h30 burn time per cube.
  • Ash controlled at 1.9–2.2% with white residue.
  • OEM packaging (inner box, master carton, private label).
Endurance 02

Hexagonal 2.2 cm (Hollow Core)

Hollow airflow extends burn duration while protecting the integrity of the briquette during long-haul shipments.

  • Optimised draft keeps combustion even throughout service.
  • Survives 8+ drop tests for export resilience.
  • Custom length options to match your standard pack sizes.
Specialty 03

Flat & Kaloud Discs

Slim discs tailored for lotus/Kaloud systems with quick ignition and smooth ember profiles for high-end lounges.

  • Gentle ember plume protects delicate flavours.
  • 100% coconut shells with low volatile matter.
  • Available in 20, 24, or custom-piece inner boxes.

How to Order

Made-to-order with export support

  1. 1. Align specifications (size, packaging, branding) and confirm MOQ of 18–19 tons (one 20-ft container).
  2. 2. Receive proforma invoice, production timeline, and artwork guide from the export desk.
  3. 3. Manufacturing, QC, and weathering occur with traceable batch documentation.
  4. 4. Final inspection, fumigation (if required), packing, and container stuffing with photographic reports.
